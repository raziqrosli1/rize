About This Scent

Inspired by Ariana Grande Sweet Like Candy, RIZÉ Lumina delivers a playful sweet feminine signature with impressive long lasting performance.

The opening reveals a fruity candy sweetness that feels soft, youthful and addictive. Within minutes, the fragrance develops into a creamy sweet floral profile that feels comforting and charming.

As it settles, Lumina melts into warm vanilla and soft musk creating a smooth lingering scent throughout the day.

With estimated 8–12 hours longevity, Lumina is one of the strongest performers in the RIZÉ line — noticeable on skin and often stays on clothing even after hours.

Perfect for daily wear, campus, night outings and anyone who loves sweet feminine signature scents.
