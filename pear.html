About This Scent

RIZÉ Pear Blossom is a fresh clean feminine scent designed for comfort and daily wear. Built with light airy sweetness, this fragrance gives a soft refreshing presence that feels effortless and modern.

The opening starts with juicy pear freshness that feels bright and uplifting. As it settles, gentle floral sweetness blends into clean musk creating a calm feminine aura.

With an estimated 3–7 hours longevity, this scent focuses on a clean personal aura rather than heavy projection. It stays noticeable in close range, perfect for those who prefer soft and comforting fragrances.
