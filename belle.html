About This Scent

Inspired by Lancome La Vie Est Belle, RIZÉ Belle Éclat delivers a soft luxurious feminine signature with balanced sweetness and elegance.

This fragrance opens with a refined floral sweetness that feels premium and feminine without being overpowering. As it settles, creamy floral accords blend beautifully to create a confident and elegant aura.

With approximately 46% perfume oil concentration, Belle Éclat offers strong performance lasting 6–8 hours on skin and longer on clothing.

Perfect for daily wear, office, date nights and special occasions. A clean feminine signature designed for modern luxury presence.
