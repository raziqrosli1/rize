About This Scent

RIZÉ Atlas delivers a bold masculine presence balanced with smooth caramel sweetness.

As it settles, Atlas develops into a smooth woody-sweet drydown creating a premium masculine aura that feels powerful yet refined.

With approximately 8–14 hours longevity, Atlas is one of the strongest performers in the RIZÉ collection — noticeable on skin and long lasting on clothing.

Perfect for night wear, dates, events and men who want a confident luxury signature scent.
